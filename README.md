# About

Sirar Salih is an experienced senior consultant with almost 20 years experience in the IT industry. Programming is his passion. Through his career, he has come to learn that effective communication is an essential part of his work and that too has become a passion of his. His mantra is; placing the end-user at the forefront of development. He loves delivering value.

Sirar considers leadership an important part of development and believes that the success of a team depends on good leadership. He believes in team effort and takes pride in working with others. He likes to share knowledge by being involved in the community. He co-organized meetups at Azure User Group Norway, he also co-founded the Angular User Group Oslo and was an active board member of the Norwegian .NET User Group Oslo from 2013 to 2020. He has spoken at international tech conferences, was interviewed by Microsoft Norway and featured on the MSDN blog in 2015. In 2018, he was interviewed by David Rael about his community engagements on his famous Developer On Fire podcast.

On May 1st 2019, Sirar received the prestigious Microsoft Most Valuable Professional award in the Azure category by Microsoft, at the time making history by becoming the third person to ever receive this award in Norway and number 401 in the world.

On April 14th 2020, Sirar founded his own technology consultancy company, Wintry Tech AS. The company was a success and quickly grew in size.
